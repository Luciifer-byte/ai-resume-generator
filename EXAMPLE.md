# Example: AI tailoring in action

This shows what `SKILL.md` does to a single highlight bullet when given a job
description that emphasizes data-driven growth marketing.

## Before (generic cv.yaml highlight)

```yaml
highlights:
  - Led integrated marketing campaigns reaching 50M+ users, coordinating with
    cross-functional business, IT and external partners.
```

## Job description excerpt (input)

```
...looking for a Growth Marketing Manager who can own campaign performance
reporting, partner closely with data/analytics teams, and scale acquisition
channels using experimentation...
```

## After (tailored by the LLM using SKILL.md)

```yaml
highlights:
  - Directed integrated growth marketing campaigns reaching 50M+ users,
    partnering with data/analytics and IT teams to run experimentation-driven
    acquisition strategies and track campaign performance end to end.
```

## Changes table (also generated by SKILL.md)

| Section | Before | After | Reason |
|---|---|---|---|
| Highlight 1 | "Led integrated marketing campaigns..." | "Directed integrated growth marketing campaigns..." | Matches "Growth Marketing Manager" title and "experimentation" language from JD |

Nothing here is invented — the underlying achievement (50M+ users, cross-functional
coordination) stays the same. Only the framing and keyword emphasis shift to match
the role.
